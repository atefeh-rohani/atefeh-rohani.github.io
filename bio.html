About Atefeh Rohani

I am an Applied Scientist and Formal Verification Engineer specializing in ML reliability for safety-critical systems. I hold a PhD in Computer Science and a CAS in Advanced Data Science and Machine Learning from the University of Bern.

Current Research & Expertise

Currently, I am a Senior Researcher in the Software Engineering Group at the University of Bern. My work is focused on advancing Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) reliability through integrated testing and formal verification. With over 6 years of experience, I have developed expertise in:

  • Architecting predictive AI models using Python, TensorFlow, and Scikit-learn.
  • Anomaly detection and specification mining for autonomous systems.
  • Runtime verification and formal specification for space software architectures.
  • Ensuring high-assurance and reliability in AI-driven safety-critical architectures.

Academic Background

  • PhD in Computer Science: Universität Bern, Switzerland.
  • M.Sc. in Soft Computing: Shahid Bahonar University of Kerman.
  • B.Sc. in Mathematics: Shahid Bahonar University of Kerman.

Personal Interests

When I am not in the lab, I am a soprano singer with the Bahar Choir, performing with the East-Paris Philharmonic Orchestra. I also enjoy hiking and biking through the Swiss Alps.
