About Atefeh Rohani

I am an Applied Scientist and Formal Verification Engineer specializing in ML reliability for safety-critical systems[cite: 1, 2, 3]. I hold a PhD in Computer Science and a CAS in Advanced Data Science and Machine Learning from the University of Bern[cite: 4, 7, 11].

Current Research & Expertise

Currently, I am a Senior Researcher in the Software Engineering Group at the University of Bern[cite: 20]. My work is focused on advancing Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) reliability through integrated testing and formal verification. With over 6 years of experience, I have developed expertise in:

  • Architecting predictive AI models using Python, TensorFlow, and Scikit-learn[cite: 5, 22].
  • Anomaly detection and specification mining for autonomous systems[cite: 27].
  • Runtime verification and formal specification for space software architectures[cite: 32, 33].
  • Ensuring high-assurance and reliability in AI-driven safety-critical architectures[cite: 28].

Academic Background

  • PhD in Computer Science: Universität Bern, Switzerland[cite: 11].
  • M.Sc. in Soft Computing: Shahid Bahonar University of Kerman[cite: 9].

Personal Interests
