Teaching Experience

I have had the privilege of serving as a Teaching Assistant for several core computer science courses, where I focus on making complex theoretical concepts accessible and engaging for students.

Advanced Algorithms

Teaching Assistant | University of Bern

Focused on complex algorithmic design, complexity analysis, and optimization techniques.

Computability and Complexity Theory (in German)

Teaching Assistant | University of Bern

Assisted students in understanding Turing machines, P vs NP problems, and the foundations of computational limits.

Justification Logic

Teaching Assistant | University of Bern

Explored formal systems for reasoning about evidence and justification in epistemic contexts.

Mentorship

In addition to classroom teaching, I actively mentor Bachelor’s and Master’s students in their research projects, particularly in areas related to Formal Verification and Reliable AI. I believe in fostering a research environment that encourages critical thinking, rigorous testing and creative methods.
